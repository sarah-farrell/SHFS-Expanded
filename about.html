About the stand

Sugar Hill Flower Stand features organic seasonal bouquets at an affordable price

Where we started

We started selling our self-serve produce stand in the Summer of 2018. It was not long before we realized that our limited floral and herb bouquets were in high demand, selling out every week. Herbs are our favorite 'filler' for both their beauty and their scent.

What we offer

We sell organic seasonal flower and herbal arrangments at our self-serve stand. Our variety of flowers is changing every year, but our commitment to quality is consistent. If it isn't perfect, it isn't for sale.

Where we're headed

Our business is growing to include formal events and resturant decor, in addition to our weekly self-serve stand. New this year; our gardens are availible by appointment to individuals or photographers looking for colorul, natural settings to caputure the perfect picture.
